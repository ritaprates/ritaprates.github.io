Storyo B2B Video

At Storyo I was tasked with making a video for a potential partnership with amusement parks, showcasing what Storyo does and how it could be a great personalised tool for brands. After being given a draft of the script by the Marketing team, I started working on a storyboard and then started reserching images; we ended up deciding to buy a group of stock photos and two videos that were useful for our purpose. I created all of the motion graphics and animations in the video, was solely responsible for editing it and oversaw the sound recording for the video (and then edited the sound files and synchronised them with the video). Making this video allowed me to realise how much my video/motion graphics skills had improved since I finished school and started working.
August/September 2016
Client: Storyo app
designed and developed by rita prates. made with love in brackets and hosted on github.
