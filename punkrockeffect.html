thepunkrockeffect.rocks

My Master's degree thesis, entitled "The punk-rock effect: an archive of the role of music in the expression of design", consists of two components: written and practical. This responsive website is the practical component and it contains the information studied in the written one.
It has two main pages that allow the user to view the collection of album covers analysed: one allows the user to explore it by dragging the covers around, simulating a physical collection of vinyl records, and the other displays the collection organised and filtered according to various parameters. When the user clicks on an album cover a pop-up window with the info about that album opens. There's also a page about each musical movement and one about the project.

You can visit the site here but it's only available in Portuguese at the moment; I am working on translating it into English.
