things that really matter

I'm pretty good at using computer programs and take pride in it. I'm proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects and Premiere Pro.

I'm happier when designing books and newspapers or designing and developing websites.

My HTML and CSS skills are pretty good, but that's pretty much where my programming/markup knowledge stops. I'd love to know programming languages, though!

Branding isn't my cup of tea, mainly because I didn't do much of it at school. However, I'm always interested in learning!

I'm strangely good at googling things; really, it's a gift.

I love typography, whether it's type design, letterpress, or simply admiring typefaces for extended periods of time.

I am fluent in Portuguese and English. I can understand and communicate very easily in Spanish. I can read and understand French, but writing and speech is a bit more difficult.
