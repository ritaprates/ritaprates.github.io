Storyo app Screenshots

Screenshots for Storyo app version 1.0 and 2.0. The 1.0 version ended up not being used, but the 2.0 version was uploaded to the App Store. The concept for the 2.0 version was created in collaboration with Leonel Duarte. After the app icon was updated the screenshots also needed to be, in order to reflect the new icon colours.
In September 2017, after the announcement of the iPhone X, we felt the need to create a new size of screenshots. We took this opportunity to create fully localized versions for 7 languages (with unique photos, maps, Shutterstock videos list and Facebook posts). In total, the screenshots are available in 15 languages, but the European and English versions use the same photos and maps.
June 2016 / February 2017 / September 2017
Client: Storyo app
designed and developed by rita prates. made with love in brackets and hosted on github.
