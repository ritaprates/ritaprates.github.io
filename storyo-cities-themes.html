Storyo app Cities Themes

Storyo is an app that transforms photos into short video memories with maps, titles, captions and more. It was frequently used by users to document trips and special events, which led to this campaign.
This is a series of themes designed for the app based on a list of cities the users visited regularly; each theme consists of an overlay that features the city name and an icon designed specifically for it.

The icons are: Barcelona - La Sagrada Familia, Berlin - Brandenburg Gate, Chicago - Chicago Theatre Marquee, Chichen Itza - El Castillo, Hanoi - Tran Quoc Pagoda, Istanbul - Galata Tower, Las Vegas - Welcome to Fabulous Las Vegas Sign, Lisboa - Tram, London - Big Ben, Madrid - Real Casa de Correos, Marrakech - Koutoubia, New York - Statue of Liberty, Paris - Eiffel Tower, Porto - Rabelo boat, Prague - Church of Our Lady before Týn, Rio de Janeiro - Cristo Redentor, Rome - Colosseum, San Francisco - Golden Gate Bridge, São Paulo - Octávio Frias de Oliveira Bridge, Siem Reap - Angkor Wat, Ubud - Pura Dalem Agung Padangtegal.
