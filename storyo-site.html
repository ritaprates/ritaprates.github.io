Storyo app Website

Complete redesign (but not development) of the Storyo app website to focus on the new 2.0 version features. The site is composed by sections that focus on one feature each and that can be expanded to read more about it.
Previously, for Storyo 1.0, I had worked on a page for a summer campaign (designed and developed), a 'blog' page and respective artcile pages (designed and developed) and an About page (designed only).
I also designed and developed the Invite page for Storyo 2.0 collaborative stories, which is sent via e-amil. This page gets the name of the story creator, their profile picture and the title, date and cover of the story directly from the app. You can see the template here.
