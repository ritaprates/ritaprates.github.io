Storyo app Welcome e-mail

This is a Welcome e-mail for Storyo app which explains the new features of version 2.0 of the app. After designing the e-mail I started working on developing it. HTML e-mails are incredibly challenging (and frustrating) to develop because of the many different e-mail clients (most of them with desktop, mobile and online versions), each one with specific limitations and different feature support.
This project was very rewarding because it was my first experience with HTML e-mails and even with all the limitations I managed to achieve the desired result.

The web version of the e-mail is available here.
