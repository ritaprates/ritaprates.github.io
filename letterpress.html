top 10 billboard album chart letterpress posters

This project was my first experiment with letterpress; after studying Design for three years it was only during my Master's degree that I had the chance to use movable type. The theme for the project was free and I decided to focus on music. To narrow it down, I chose to do some lyric posters from albums that reached the Top 10 on the Billboard album chart. This project was based on a lot of experimentation with type and materials for the posters; these are the results. With letterpress it's very easy to make a mistake, especially on the first few projects, so I think it's interesting to keep the posters that have mistakes in them.
