The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy Movie Website

This website is the result of a Webdesign university project, in which students were asked to create a website for a movie that didn't have one. I chose the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy. On the second semester we had to turn our existing desktop website into a responsive one, so this is the final result. You can visit the website at hitchhikersguide.ga, but please keep in mind that since this was a university project, a free hosting service was used, and as such the site takes a while to load properly. This was my first experience with webdesign, HTML and CSS.
January/June 2015
