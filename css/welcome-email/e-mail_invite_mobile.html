We're really happy that you're here! Storyo 2.0 is the world's only collaborative storytelling app that collects and combines everyone's photos, Facebook posts, maps and other amazing details to document and celebrate life experiences in long-lasting video memories to review and relive.

Here's a quick rundown of the new features:


Create together!

Only Storyo 2.0 gives you the ability to create amazing group video memories with unique details by joining photos and perspectives from everyone that shared one moment. Storyo 2.0 helps you deliver a better story of your life’s most magical moments.

Easily invite your friends to be a part of your story and let Storyo gather everyone’s best photos and incorporate them into your video memory. The more perspectives you include, the easier it is to recapture the best of any experience.



Relive every last detail

Log in with Facebook and Storyo 2.0 will mix in posts that best express the emotions of a special moment. And to remind you how hot, cold or perfect it was, Storyo 2.0 can include the weather.




Let a professional in on your story

Add a professional touch to your stories with stunning videos from a specially curated collection by Shutterstock. Storyo 2.0 automatically suggests video clips that seem best suited. Pick the one you like best and the next thing you know, it’s a part of your video.
